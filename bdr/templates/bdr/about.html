About the Biological Data Repository

Research in the field of bioinformatics depends heavily upon biological data compiled in various datasets. While often public, these datasets vary considerably in format, access mechanisms, release frequency, and in the availability of previous releases. This latter point is of note as the ability to use old data to verify predictions in light of more recent updates offers a potent way to evaluate new methods.

This repository retains prior revisions for such datasets which not only centralises these data sources, but also enables verification regardless of whether historical data is maintained by the original publisher. Given the variance in release schedules among datasets, updates are captured automatically to prevent the loss of data.
